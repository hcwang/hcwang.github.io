瞻仰

2003-10-20 by 开心就好

注：小马哥提醒我，有可能阅读本Blog的人不知道闫诺是何许人也，甚至认为闫诺就是开心就好，所以在这儿声明一下，闫诺是本届MVP中年龄最小的一个，不是开心，呵呵:P

闫诺发给Bill G的去信：

Dear Mr. Gates,

Microsoft (Shanghai) GTEC held a wonderful China MVP Summit 2003 on 9/26.
The agenda of the summit was:
1.       Lecture by Microsoft Vice President Sean O'Driscoll, introduced Microsoft Communities and MVP Program.
2.       Lecture by Dr. Kai-Fu Lee, introduced how to be a great technical expert in Digital Decade.
3.       Lecture by Mr. Peterson, introduced and demonstrated Microsoft Longhorn OS.
4.       Awarding to new China MVPs.
5.       Technical Sessions.
6.       Q & A about Microsoft Communities (by Sean O'Driscoll).

We really had a wonderful time at the summit. Attached please find the photos we took there.

I'm the youngest MVP in China. Now I'm a high school student and preparing to apply university.

Thanks Microsoft for the great summit.

With Best Regards,
Nuo Yan

Bill G Office的回信：

Dear Nuo,

Thank you for your kind email to Bill Gates letting him know you enjoyed
your time at the China MVP Summit.  It's very thoughtful of you to share
the great photographs you captured and we thank you for your
contributions as Microsoft's youngest MVP.  We wish you continued
success and all the best as you begin to send your applications to
universities.

Kind regards,
Emily Byrne
Office

另外，在博客盟中增加了MVPs Weblogs(U.S.)，是美国MVP Leader Jerry主办的。
